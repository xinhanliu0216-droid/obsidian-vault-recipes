# Wiki Formal

Create an auditable draft. Do not imply that Agent review replaces legal, academic, financial, safety, or organizational approval.

## Confirm the brief

Clarify:

- Audience and decision
- Required format and length
- Deadline and review owner
- Citation style
- Whether draft files may be created

## Ground the draft

1. Read `CLAUDE.md`, `index.md`, relevant synthesis, and Source pages.
2. Build a claim-to-source outline before prose.
3. Exclude unsupported claims or label them as pending verification.
4. Preserve material counterevidence and meaningful alternatives.

## Draft

- Match the requested genre instead of applying one universal report template.
- Cite the supporting Wiki or Source page near important claims.
- Keep internal confidence metadata out of polished prose unless the audience needs it.
- Put unresolved items in a visible review section.
- Never invent figures, quotations, citations, approvals, or stakeholder positions.

## Pre-submission review

Report:

- Claims checked against sources
- Unresolved facts
- Conflicting evidence
- Sensitive information included
- Required human reviewers

Only label output “final” after the named human reviewer approves it.
